Sending Emails

Send emails using an alias by authetnicating with the SMTP server.

Gmail

To send an email as hello@example.net from your Gmail account through SMTP server mail.example.com.

  1. Add a new entry for the alias for the in the users table.

  2. Go to the Accounts and Import settings page.

  3. Under “Send mail as,” click “Add another email address.”

  4. Enter the email alias and click “Next Step &gt;&gt;”.

  1. Enter the credentials.

  2. An email will be sent to hello@example.net with a confirmation code.

  3. Done!
